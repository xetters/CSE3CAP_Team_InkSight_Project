Implementation and Features

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript handles all file uploads and displays analysis results
  • Backend: Node.js and Express serve the application and manage API requests
  • Python Processing: Python scripts use NLTK (Natural Language Toolkit) for tokenization and text processing. NLTK is free and open source with no copyright restrictions
  • NLP Libraries: FastText provides advanced word embeddings for sentiment and semantic analysis. FastText is free and open source, so we can host it on our own servers without licensing fees or data sharing concerns
  • Unlike large language models run on external servers (which may cost money and send your data elsewhere), our NLP tools are self-hosted and completely transparent

Word Analysis

  • Identifies the most frequently used words throughout your text
  • Helps you understand your vocabulary choices and repetition patterns
  • Displays word counts and frequency rankings for easy identification of dominant terms

Keyness Statistics

  • Tokenizes text into individual words, converts to lowercase, filters to alphabetic characters only
  • Counts word frequencies across the entire text using NLTK tokenization
  • Frequency analysis: scores words by occurrence count divided by total word count
  • Comparative mode available: uses log-likelihood calculation to compare your text against a reference corpus
  • Automatically filters out words below minimum length thresholds, rare occurrences, and insignificant scores
  • Returns ranked top keywords sorted by significance score for deep insight into your distinctive writing patterns

Semantic Analysis

  • Groups semantically related words using FastText word embeddings to reveal thematic patterns in your writing
  • Uses KMeans clustering to automatically organize words into meaningful semantic groups
  • Identifies dominant themes by analyzing which word clusters are most prevalent in your text
  • Displays top words from each cluster, showing relationships between concepts and ideas
  • Helps writers understand the conceptual landscape of their work and discover recurring themes
