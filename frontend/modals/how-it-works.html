How It Works

What is InkSight?

  • A text analysis tool for writers to understand patterns and characteristics in their writing
  • Completely free, open source, and requires no account creation

Privacy

  • All processing happens in-memory only, with no data transmitted to external servers
  • Your text is never stored on any server or database
  • Data is automatically cleared when you refresh the page, close the browser, or click the Clear Results button
  • Our NLP tools run on our own servers using commercial-license-free, open-source libraries, so there are no third-party data-sharing agreements or licensing restrictions
  • We have zero access to your writing at any point

Data Flow

  • You can upload a file (TXT, DOCX, MD, or ODT) or paste text directly into the editor
  • Your text is extracted and processed in-memory only on your browser
  • The results will then be displayed instantly for your review
  • You can download your analysis results as an HTML file, which can also be saved to PDF
